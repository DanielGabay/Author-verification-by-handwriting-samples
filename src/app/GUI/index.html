The program compares between two hebrew handwritten documents and display how similar they are.
The program is intended for use by the National Institute for Testing & Evaluation to identify frauds in exams.

How it works

Each document is processed in order to identify the letters in the text.
After the program finds and identify some of the letters, they than compared
by our three algorithms which at the end returns a score in precentage.

File selection

1) At the Files Selection window, browse two files that you want to compare.
2) After the files are loaded, click on the 'Compare' button to initiate the system.
3) View the results on the 'Results' window.

Folder selection

1) At the Folder Selection window, browse a folder that contains pairs of files that will be compared.
2) After the folder is loaded, click on the 'Compare' button to initiate the system.
3) After each pair of documents is done, you can view thier results by choosing
them at the dropdown menu on the 'Results' window.
4) At any time you can click the 'Stop' button to stop the comparision (you canno't resume afterwards).
5) At any time you can also save the results to an excel file by clicking 'Save Results' button on the results window.
